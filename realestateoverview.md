📌 Project: AI-Powered System for Real Estate

1. Intelligent Lead Scoring
   Objective: Automatically prioritize leads based on potential.

Advanced tracking of online behavior: time spent on each property listing, zooming on specific images, repeated searches in specific areas.

Segmentation through demographic data, geolocation, and on-site interactions.

AI-based scoring engine that dynamically updates lead scores in real-time.

2. Advanced Conversational Chatbot (Web + WhatsApp + Social Media)
   Objective: Qualify and manage inbound leads autonomously.

NLP-powered chatbot (not pre-set replies), capable of handling complex conversations.

Features:

Lead qualification through personalized questions.

Answering technical queries about properties.

Booking visits directly into the calendar.

Collecting detailed preferences (budget, location, property type).

3. Automated Property Valuation & Predictive Pricing
   Objective: Deliver accurate valuations and predictive price recommendations.

Analyzes:

Land registry data and past transactions.

Structural property attributes.

Market trends and local infrastructure.

Sentiment analysis from neighborhood reviews.

Predictive pricing engine that suggests optimal listing prices and timing based on seasonality, local events, and macroeconomic dynamics.

4. Targeted Email Marketing System
   Objective: Display properties tailored to the user's profile.

Utilizes our internal database (segmented by family size and income).

Automatic matching with suitable properties by budget, area, and household composition.

AI engine suggests and automates email campaigns based on user preferences and behavior.

5. Predictive Dashboard (AI-Integrated CRM)
   Objective: Centralized KPI and forecasting hub.

Real-time insights into:

Market trends.

Agent performance.

Deal pipeline with closing probability.

Cross-selling and up-selling opportunities.

6. Forecasting & Marketing Planning System
   Objective: Strategic planning for marketing investment and resource allocation.

Sales volume predictions.

Identification of optimal marketing periods.

Budget optimization based on seasonal and market data.

7. Integration with External Real Estate Portals
   Objective: Collect data from online sources and social media in real time.

Automatic notifications for saved searches from:

Immobiliare.it (REST-XML API).

Idealista.it (OAuth2 API, JSON format).

OpenAPI (Quotazioni Start) for pricing and registry data.

Scraping integration (where allowed) from:

Tecnocasa, Casa.it, Subito.it, Bakeca.it, Trovacasa.net, Ccasa.it, Mitula.it, Trovit.it.

Social monitoring of Facebook groups and real estate communities for buyer/seller leads.
